Product Association Campaign

A leading retailer wanted to develop a marketing strategy that enabled predictable forecasting and increased market share.

Company

Large Retailer

Industry

Retail

Service

Data Analysis

Technologies

Python, SQL

{{< img src="competition.png" class="img-fluid img-thumbnail" >}}

The Problem

As the market grew, more competitors began to enter the market. They wanted to find a solution that allowed them to maintain a competitive advantage.

{{< img src="products.png" class="img-fluid img-thumbnail" >}}

The Solution

By analyzing the data and understanding correlations between products, a unique algorithm was created. Using product association rules as the fundamental basis behind the model, we predicted which customers were likely to buy products based on their purchasing of associated products.

{{< img src="customer-segment.png" class="img-fluid img-thumbnail" >}}

Results

The delivery of this project was the segment of customers that were most likely to purchase the products. The retailer targeted the provided segment and ran email marketing campaigns with coupons for the targeted products. This campaign became a regular marketing strategy for the retailer.
