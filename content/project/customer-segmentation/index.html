Customer Segmentation

A retailer wanted to get a deeper understanding of their customers to run targeted campaigns.

Company

Retailer

Industry

Retail

Service

Data Analysis

Technologies

Python, SQL

The Problem

The retailer possessed a large amount of data but wanted to understand their customers’ buying behaviour in order to maximize the return of their marketing investment.

The Solution

We built customer segments using clustering algorithms that categorized customers in key categories such as: how recently they made a purchase, how frequently they buy, and how much they spent over a period of time (RFM Segmentation).

We helped the retailer understand the behaviours of their core members and monitor attrition and acquisition by designing a series of routine reports based on this segmentation. Thereafter, this segmentation was further developed for targeted campaigns.

Results

Several successful targeted campaigns were ran based on this customer segmentation. As a result, our client completed adapted our customer segmentation as part of their business evaluation process.

On top of the RFM segment, we further developed a marketing focused member evaluation model to show and predict each member’s value and potential.
