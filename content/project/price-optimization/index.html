Price Optimization

A retailer wanted to determine the right price to charge for a particular category of products.

Company

Large Retailer

Industry

Retail

Service

Data Analysis

Technologies

Python, SQL

The Problem

The retailer wanted to determine the optimal price for their high-end products so that it resulted in the most revenue without jeopardizing on amount of sales.

The Solution

We leveraged the historical data points from fluctuating prices and determined how varying different price points affected the amount of people that purchased.

Results

Our team created an algorithm that determined the optimal price point within its specific product category that resulted in the highest margins.
